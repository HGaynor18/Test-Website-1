Throttle Control Mastery

Proper throttle control is critical for smooth acceleration and deceleration. Here’s how to master it:

Step 1: Gentle Throttle Engagement

When starting, apply gentle pressure to the throttle. Jerky movements can make the bike lurch, so focus on smooth, controlled inputs.

Step 2: Throttle Roll-On

As you begin moving, slowly roll on the throttle to increase speed. Practice on a straight path to get a feel for how much throttle your bike needs.

Step 3: Maintain a Steady Speed

Once you've reached your desired speed, maintain a consistent throttle. Avoid excessive or frequent adjustments to keep the bike stable.

Step 4: Deceleration

When slowing down, gradually ease off the throttle. Sudden closure can cause the bike to lurch forward, disrupting your balance.

Disclaimer: Throttle responsibly. The faster you go, the harder you fall—so keep the speed within your comfort zone!
