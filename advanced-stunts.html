Advanced Stunts

Ready to take your riding to the next level? Here are some advanced stunts you can practice to really show off your skills.

Step 1: The Endo (Stoppie)

Bring the bike to a rolling stop while applying the front brake, causing the rear wheel to lift off the ground. Timing and balance are crucial here.

Step 2: The Rolling Burnout

While holding the front brake, rev the throttle and let the rear wheel spin, creating a controlled burnout. This is not for the faint of heart!

Step 3: Power Wheelies

Unlike clutch-ups, power wheelies are achieved purely by applying throttle. Find the sweet spot in your throttle control to bring the front wheel up without overdoing it.

Disclaimer: Just because you can, doesn’t mean you should. Advanced stunts are risky, and unless you’re riding pro, keep it chill and safe!
